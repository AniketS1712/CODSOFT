BCIIT Information Portal Project

This project is developed using Django and serves as an information portal for BCIIT. The portal includes various features such as home page, staff information, events, student details, and forms.

Home Page

The home page provides an overview of the portal and welcomes users to the BCIIT information system.

Staff Information

This section contains details about the staff members at BCIIT, including their roles and contact information.

Staff Temporary Information

This section temporarily displays information about staff members, which might be used during updates or data migration.

Events

The events section showcases upcoming and past events organized by BCIIT.

Student Information

This section provides details about the students at BCIIT, including their profiles and academic information.

Forms

The forms section includes various forms that students and staff can fill out for different purposes.
